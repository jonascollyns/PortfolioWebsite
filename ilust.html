Below you can find some of my Illustrations that I have posted on Instagram (my main way of promoting myself online). I started creating digital art in 2018 while i was still in highschool and I began taking classes for it. I took a year to focus on my art that I was making and began to become a freelancing digital artist for music artists in US, New Zealand and Europe. Throughout time I have changed and experimented with different styles in my art. An example of this is my beginning artworks being very simple non-shaded artworks that didn't feel unique. I then found two artists by the name of Kodone and Kill_Che when I began to define my own style by making my art work alot more dark and messy. I find my most unique style to my art is how I make my line art; as usually it's supposed to be clean and one line but I make mine as messy as possible without being too overloading to the eyes.
